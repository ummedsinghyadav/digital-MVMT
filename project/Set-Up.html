Starting a business requires more than just a great idea

You need to ensure everything is legally structured and compliant. We take care of the paperwork, registrations, and requirements so you don’t have to worry about missing a critical step.

Why your site isn’t converting as it should:

  • LLC Formation

    We’ll register your Limited Liability Company (LLC) in any state, ensuring it’s structured properly to protect your personal assets and provide tax flexibility. Whether you're a solo entrepreneur or forming a multi-member business, we handle everything.

  • EIN Registration

    An Employer Identification Number (EIN) is required for tax filings, hiring employees, and opening a business bank account. We’ll obtain your EIN from the IRS, so you’re set up for success from day one.

  • Registered Agent Services

    Most states require a registered agent to receive legal documents and government notices on your behalf. We provide a reliable, compliant registered agent service to keep your business in good standing.

  • Licenses & Permits

    Many industries require specific licenses and permits at the local, state, or federal level. We research, file, and secure the necessary approvals to keep your business compliant and avoid costly fines.

  • Sales Tax Permit


    If you’re selling products or services that require sales tax collection, you’ll need a sales tax permit. We handle the registration process, ensuring you can legally collect and remit sales tax in your state.

  • Business Insurance Assistance


    Every business faces risks, from liability claims to property damage. We help you secure the right business insurance to protect your company, assets, and reputation. Whether it’s general liability, professional liability, or workers’ compensation, we’ll guide you to the right policy.
